Ramen Distribution:
Once the data was read into Pandas, the first aspect I wanted to analyze was how the star reviews were distributed. I choose to use a Histogram to seperate the Stars (Reviews) into 6 bins. The output of this graph indicates that of the 2580 total ramen Reviews, the majority have reviewed in the 3.5 - 4.0 range. This information suggests that instant ramen is a very popular food that is modestly reviewed by many countries and cultures around the world.
