Popular Styles of Ramen in USA:
The next part of my analysis focused on the United States. The first thing I wanted to take a closer look at was what type of ramen was preferred in the United States. After grouping the DataFrame by Style of Ramen, I used a bar plot to return the results. What I found was that the Pack style of ramen was by far the most popular style the United States, followed by moderate reviews of Bowl, Cups and Trays. I was not surpised to find that the Pack syle was the most reviewed style since it is an item that can be purchased in bulk and is an easy and quick meal. The bottom 3 brands (Bar, Box, Can) had little to no reviews from the United States. This information would suggest that these styles of Ramen are not as available or assessible in the United States which is why they had little reviews.
